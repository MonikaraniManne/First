Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering (SVCE) is an engineering college in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 2007, it is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur (JNTUA), and is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi.Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering, Tirupati was established in 2007. It is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur. The institute is spread over an area of 10 acres. SVCE Tirupati is approved by AICTE. It is accredited by NAAC with an ‘A’ grade. The total number of faculty members there in the college is 291 and the total student enrollment is 4309 students. The college offers B.Tech, M.Tech, M.B.A and M.C.A courses in engineering, computer application and IT, animation and design, sciences and management and business administration streams.
